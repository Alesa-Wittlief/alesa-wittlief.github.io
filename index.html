Entrepreneur and Explorer

As a soloprenuer, I have a naturally adventurous spirit and a passion for living life to the fullest. I try to live my life in such a way that I give back as much as I can to the world around me. I have a strong desire to learn and grow until the day I die. I'd love to see as much of the world as possible, as well. Only by understanding cultures outside of our own can we truly love and grow as human beings.

Before transitioning into learning coding, I had a modestly successful career as a coffee shop owner. Little did I know the value of community and connection until I built my business around it. While owning the coffeehouse, I learned how to impact my community more by hosting fundraisers for those in medical need, or by organizing art-night patio events that grew to an old-town, monthly event with bigger bands and brought in business to my neighbors, too. While I loved owning the coffee shop, I yearned for other experiences - to be free to travel and to also try Real Estate!
